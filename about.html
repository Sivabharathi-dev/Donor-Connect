About Us

Donor Connect - A unique societal platform, which facilitates real-time connect between the requester and the willing blood donor in a defined geography.

The workflow is to connect the willing donor to save a life at times of need. Donor Connect -a web platform to find blood donors online.

The application enables real-time connections and notifications and streamlining the process of finding donors in times of emergency like never before.

We are reaching out to the community with a simple request-to help us signing up donors to Donor Connect. This will be another small step in continuation with your contribution to building a better society.

Moral Principles that guides Our Website

Emphasizing the selfless act of donating blood to save lives.

Highlighting the importance of responding to the needs of those in critical situations.

Encouraging individuals to take an active role in supporting public health and saving lives through regular blood donations.

Ensuring the website and donation process are accessible and welcoming to all,regardless of background or ability.
